📖 How ASR 7-Agent System Works

Each agent handles a specific part of the recruitment pipeline. Run them individually or all together for a complete daily cycle.

1
🔍 Lead Generation — Discovers companies actively hiring. Scans LinkedIn, Google Maps, Naukri, and Indeed for BPO/IT companies in target cities. Collects company name, HR contact, role needed, and salary range. Output: New leads appear in the Leads tab.
2
📧 Email Outreach — Takes new leads and sends personalized messages. Uses email for corporate contacts and WhatsApp for direct HR numbers. Tracks open rates and responses. Output: Lead status changes from 'new' to 'contacted'.
3
📅 Meeting Booking — Monitors outreach responses. When an HR replies positively, automatically proposes meeting slots and books calls. Output: New meetings appear in the Meetings tab.
4
👤 Candidate Intake — Collects candidate profiles from WhatsApp groups, Google Forms, job portal applications, and referrals. Adds them to the central database with contact info and basic skills. Output: New candidates in the Candidates tab.
5
📄 Resume Parser & Scorer — AI reads each candidate's resume, extracts skills, experience, and location. Assigns a 0-100 AI score based on overall quality. Output: Candidates get AI scores and summaries.
6
🎯 AI Matching — When a client needs specific candidates (e.g., "BPO Voice Lucknow 15K"), this agent searches the database and ranks the best matches. Use the search bar to trigger this. Output: Ranked match results.
7
🗓️ Interview Scheduler — Takes top-matched candidates and schedules interviews. Sends WhatsApp invites to candidates with date, time, and company details. Output: Candidate status changes to 'interview_scheduled'.

💡 Tips for Best Results

✨
Run "All Agents" once daily for a complete pipeline cycle. This runs all 7 agents in sequence.
✨
Use AI Match whenever a client sends a requirement. Type the role, city, salary in the search bar.
✨
Check Leads tab to see which companies are hiring. Contact promising ones that agents haven't reached yet.
✨
The system grows — every time you run agents, more data accumulates. The AI matching gets better with more candidates.
