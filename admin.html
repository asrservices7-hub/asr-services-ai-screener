Command Directives & End-to-End Workflow

1. Sourcing The Demand (B2B Sales)

The Lead Generation Agent scans platforms to find companies currently hiring. The Email Outreach Agent then automatically pitches your ASR screening service to them.

2. Closing The Deal

When an HR replies with interest, the Meeting Booking Agent negotiates time slots and drops a booked call directly onto your calendar. Your job: Show up and close the contract.

3. Building The Supply (B2C Talent)

Now that you have the contract, the Candidate Intake Agent pulls resumes from WhatsApp, portals, and referrals. The Resume Parser reads all of them instantly, assigning a 0-100 AI quality score.

4. Fulfilling The Order

Use the AI Matching Agent to type in exactly what the client wants (e.g., "Customer Support Noida 2 years exp"). It instantly ranks your talent pool. Finally, the Interview Scheduler Agent messages the selected candidates to set up their interviews with the client.

The Daily Routine

To let the machine do the heavy lifting, simply press the Execute Full Daily Automation Cycle button once a day. Sit back and watch the activity log as it runs all 7 steps automatically, generating sales meetings and processing resumes while you drink coffee.
